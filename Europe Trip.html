Trip Overview

In the Spring of 2017 I set off to Europe in hopes of finding good food, beer, and culture. I started in the British Isles, then on to southern Europe, where I worked my way along the Mediterranean from Spain to Italy. There I took a puddle-hopper to Greece and finally to Macedonia. I stayed exclusively in youth hostels, and traveled by train or bus.

Some Trip Highlights

  • Climbing the dome of the St. Peter's Basilica in the Vatican City
  • Staying out in Barcelona until the clubs closed (6am!)
  • Seeing the Statue of David in Florence, Italy
  • Drinking 50 cent beers in Macedonia
